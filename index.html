ABOUT

An open initiative to map building heat readiness in Amsterdam. This project visualizes physical building characteristics to identify dwellings at risk from rising urban temperatures.

AMS INSTITUTE GEMEENTE AMSTERDAM MADE LIVING LAB

MISSION ONGOING

Identify physical characteristics that increase heat vulnerability in Amsterdam buildings. Collect and integrate open data at building level enabling data-driven decisions about urban heat mitigation.

STATUS

Ongoing project: characteristics still to be mapped, coverage includes approximately 52,000 buildings predominantly in the city center.
